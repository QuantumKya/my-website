Lingo 2 Puzzle Maker

Welcome to Lingo 2 Puzzle Maker !

This is for rendering Lingo 2 Puzzles. Don't worry, it's simple!

Step 1. Enter the clue and the answer.

Step 2. Choose how many glyphs you want (only up to 4 a.t.m.)

Step 3. Configure your glyphs however you want. Only up to 4 dots allowed, though.

Step 4. Export your puzzle and share it !
