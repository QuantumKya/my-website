Lingo 2 Puzzle Viewer

by QuantumKya

Instructions!!! :3

Step 1. Make a puzzle in Lingo Maker 2

Step 2. Press the copy button

Step 3. Share your puzzle for people to solve!

If you want to make a set of puzzles, make them all, copy their data, and put them in a .txt file, line after line, in the order you want. Then share !

Welcome to Lingo 2 Puzzle Viewer !

Just upload a file (should be .txt) or paste in base64, and you have a puzzle to play!

Press left and right to change which panel you're looking at.

Controls are exactly like in Lingo 2, when it comes to typing and hint-getting.
